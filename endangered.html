What Are Endangered Species?

Endangered species are those that are at serious risk of extinction in the near future. These species face threats like habitat destruction, poaching, climate change, and loss of food sources. Protecting endangered animals is crucial to maintaining biodiversity and the balance of ecosystems. With focused conservation efforts, we can help prevent these species from disappearing forever.
